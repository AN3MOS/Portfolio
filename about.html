01   About me

I am a Computer Engineering graduate with hands-on experience in embedded systems, IoT development, robotics, and computer networking. I have worked with multiple microcontroller platforms including ESP32, Arduino Uno, Arduino Mega, Arduino Nano, and Raspberry Pi, developing systems that integrate sensors, automation, and wireless communication.

I have practical experience in building and integrating hardware-controlled applications using MIT App Inventor, enabling real-time monitoring and control of embedded systems such as RC cars and IoT-based devices. This has strengthened my ability to combine hardware engineering with mobile application development.

In robotics, I have developed and assembled drone systems, gaining experience in system integration, electronics troubleshooting, and performance tuning, which enhanced my understanding of real-world embedded system behavior and control.

During my internship as a WiFi technician, I gained hands-on experience in fiber optic handling and termination, including fiber preparation, handling, and field installation practices. This strengthened my technical discipline, attention to detail, and exposure to real-world ISP operations.

In computer networking, I completed academic training in RJ45 termination, patch panel installation, structured cabling, and fiber optic fundamentals. I also hold a Cisco certification, which strengthened my understanding of networking concepts, routing fundamentals, and network configuration principles.

I am seeking opportunities where I can apply and further develop my skills in embedded systems, IoT, robotics, and networking — contributing to practical and efficient engineering solutions in a professional environment.
